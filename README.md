# Ant Habit Tracker 🐜

A private, open-source, self-hosted habit tracker focused on simplicity, consistency, and full ownership of your data.

> [!WARNING]
> Ant Habit Tracker is currently in **alpha**.
>
> Features may change, bugs may exist, and some functionality may be incomplete.  
> Feedback, testing, and contributions are highly appreciated.

## Features

- Weekly and yearly habit tracking views.
- Mark habits as completed or skipped while keeping a full progress history.
- Streak tracking to maintain consistency and build momentum.
- Visual graphs and statistics for tracking long-term progress.
- Archive habits without losing previous records or history.
- Clean and focused interface designed for everyday use.
- Responsive design with light and dark theme support.
- Multi-language support with English and Spanish translations.
